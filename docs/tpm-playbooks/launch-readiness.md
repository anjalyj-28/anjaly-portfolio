# Launch Readiness Playbook

## Core Questions

- Is the product technically ready?
- Are dependencies closed?
- Are risks understood?
- Are owners assigned?
- Are rollback plans defined?
- Are success metrics instrumented?

## Launch Readiness Checklist

| Area | Question |
|---|---|
| Engineering | Are critical bugs closed? |
| Data | Are metrics validated? |
| Privacy | Are compliance reviews complete? |
| Support | Are escalation paths ready? |
| Infra | Is capacity validated? |
| Product | Are launch criteria approved? |

## TPM Role

The TPM owns cross-functional alignment, risk visibility, decision cadence, and launch confidence.
